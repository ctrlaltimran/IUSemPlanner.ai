# How to Avoid Schedule Clashes on IULMS

// iqra university · timetable optimization

There is nothing worse during Iqra University course registration than carefully selecting your subjects, only to realize that your classes overlap or you are stuck on campus from 8:30 AM to 5:30 PM with massive dead gaps.

Because the standard IULMS interface just lists courses in a table, spotting time clashes requires manual mapping. Here is how to fix it.

Instantly see your visual timetable
Skip the manual drawing. Use IUSemPlanner.ai to import your IULMS courses. It automatically generates a visual weekly timetable and flags overlaps in red.

## The Manual Way: Mapping Timings

If you want to build your schedule manually before hitting register on IULMS, follow these steps:

  • Step 1: Check Prerequisites. Make sure you have cleared the required courses for the subjects you want. If a prerequisite is locked, IULMS will block registration anyway.
  • Step 2: Draw a Grid. Grab a piece of paper. Write Monday to Friday across the top, and hours (8 AM to 6 PM) down the side.
  • Step 3: Block out timings. Write down the exact start and end times for the theory classes AND the labs. Labs are usually the biggest cause of overlaps.

### Watch out for "Dead Hours"

A clash isn't just when classes overlap. A "Trap Schedule" is when you have a class at 8:30 AM, and your next class isn't until 2:30 PM. You lose your entire day waiting on campus. Try to group your classes back-to-back or with a maximum 1-hour lunch break.
