# IULMS Student Portal: 5 Hacks You Need to Know

// iqra university · tips, tricks & shortcuts

The Iqra University IULMS portal is where you spend half your degree—checking grades, crying over attendance, and fighting for course registration.

While the system can feel a bit clunky, there are a few hidden tricks and workflows that every IU student should know to survive the semester.

1. The 80% Attendance Danger Zone

Iqra University is notorious for its strict attendance policy. Do not wait until the week before midterms to check your portal. In the IULMS dashboard, constantly monitor the Attendance section. If a course dips to 79%, you risk being debarred from the final exam. Always keep a buffer for emergencies.

2. The "Electives" Tab Trap

When checking your Student Information Center (SIC) course list, your core courses are neatly organized from Semester 1 to 8. However, many students forget that Electives (DE courses) are hidden at the very bottom of the page. Check this section early to plan which electives match your major (like choosing AI vs. Game Development).

3. Self-Serve Fee Vouchers

Lost the email from the finance department? You don't need to stand in line at the accounts office. You can generate and print your current semester fee voucher directly from the Finance section of the IULMS portal to avoid late fees.

4. Faculty Timetables for Counseling

Need an extension on an assignment but your professor isn't replying to emails? Use the portal to check the Faculty Timetable. You can see exactly which room they are teaching in and catch them right after their class ends.

5. The "1-Click Schedule" Hack (The Best Secret)

The hardest part of using IULMS is planning your schedule during registration without creating time clashes or massive gaps. To fix this, IU students built a free browser tool that upgrades the portal.
