// iqra university · manual calculation vs. 1-click tool

If you are an Iqra University student using the IULMS student portal, you know that the Student Information Center shows your grades, but tracking your overall CGPA across 8 semesters can be confusing.

In this guide, we'll show you exactly how to calculate your GPA manually, and how to use a free tool to do it automatically in 10 seconds.

## The Manual Way: Calculating IULMS GPA

To calculate your GPA, you need two pieces of information from your IULMS course list for every subject you've taken:

  • Credit Hours (Cr. Hrs): Usually 3 for main courses, 1 for labs.
  • Grade Points: The numerical value of your letter grade.

### Step 1: Find your Grade Points

Iqra University uses a standard 4.0 scale. Convert your letters to numbers:

A = 4.00
A- = 3.67
B+ = 3.33
B = 3.00
C+ = 2.33
C = 2.00
F = 0.00

### Step 2: The Formula

For each course, multiply the Credit Hours by the Grade Points. Add all those numbers together, and divide by the total number of credit hours you have attempted.

CGPA = (Total Quality Points) ÷ (Total Credit Hours)
